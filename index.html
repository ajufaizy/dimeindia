Pure Water for all.

Dime Klear is on a mission to provide our services all across India and across the globe, regardless of the clientele being urban or rural. We provide community water purifiers that purify water for as low as five paise per litre. We also guarantee five times less water wastage compared to the leading water purifying systems in India, which generate approximately three litres of wastewater for every one litre of pure water. Our services include : Dime Klear Community Water Purifier, Dime Klear Household Water Filter, Dime Klear Filter Water Bottle.

Dime Klear Community Water Purifier

Dime Klear’s nanotechnology based Community Water Purifying System developed and tested in the labs of the ISRO will be set up as per requirement. For most of rural India that still rely on pots, water purifiers are not an economic choice, but a luxury. Our goal is the inclusiveness of rural India to the project, without leaving them out like in the majority of technological advancements that benefit only the urban populace. With our unique concept of filter, which would be cost-efficient and impactful for rural parts of the country, we are set out to fulfil our mission. We envision a combination of our innovative water filter and the traditional water pot, which would be a game-changer. We collaborate with the Government, microfinance companies, NGOs and Corporations to reach out to the Rural Markets, which inturn would help us work at the ground level in an efficient and effective manner. As a community that is devoted to social empowerment and development without compromising the environment, Dime Klear group is committed to deliver sustainable water treatment solutions across the country and the world. Our environment friendly and hazard free technology that allows purification of water in an organic manner, works on gravity and doesn’t require electricity.



  • Low cost:Due to cost-effective technology and collaboration with the Government
  • Low maintenance:Doesn’t require filter change in every six months. Filters can be reactivated and reused.
  • Less Wastage: Five times less water wastage compared to existing water purifying systems in India. Doesn’t require electricity.
